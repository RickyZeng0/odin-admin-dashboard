Souls
There are two types of souls - currency and stat enhancements. The first is used to increase the level of the character and all companions. Souls are obtained after killing an enemy or using the souls of dead people. To increase stats: Green, Purple, Red, Blue, Yellow, Ashen, Black and Tainted Black Souls. They can be found at locations or purchased.
Sin
Doing reprehensible things, such as killing innocents or kidnapping women blackens soul with "Sin". If Sin is high enough, this will affect your standing with certain characters, and have the vengeful Black Trial chase after you. Having Sin also makes it impossible to unlock some endings.
Bonfire
Bonfires are used to restore health and mana, as well as for quick teleportation between locations. Unlike Dark Souls, bonfires do not save the player.
Skills
There are two types of skills - Mastery and Sorcery. Skills can cause damage to the enemy or inflict some effect on them or the entire team, restore HP or mana, and much more.
